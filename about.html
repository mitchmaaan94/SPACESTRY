About SPACESTRY.

SPACESTRY is an interior design studio founded in 2022 by Ayesha Agarwal, trained in Interior Architecture & Design.

We create spaces that are simple, functional, and quietly refined—guided by material, light, and proportion. Each project is shaped around its people, resulting in interiors that feel natural, personal, and at ease.
