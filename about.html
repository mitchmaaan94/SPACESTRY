About SPACESTRY.

SPACESTRY is an interior design studio founded in 2022 by Ayesha Agarwal, trained in Interior Architecture & Design.

The studio approaches each project with clarity and purpose. We take pride in creating spaces that are simple, functional, and quietly refined. A considered use of materials, light, and proportion defines the work, allowing each element to feel natural and cohesive. With a strong emphasis on understanding people, every space is shaped around its users. SPACESTRY delivers interiors that feel personal, balanced, and at ease.
