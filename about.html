Principal Designer

ID Ayesha Agarwal —
BA Hons. Interior Architecture & Design

Ayesha founded Spacestry with a single conviction: that a well-designed space changes how you feel inside it. Drawing on her decade long experience in the field, she brings together considered materiality, spatial restraint, and a deep understanding of the lives that unfold within every room she designs.

Her practice moves between residential and commercial work — from intimate homes in Ahmedabad to curated hospitality environments — always seeking that still point where form and feeling meet.

Our Philosophy

Restraint over excess

We believe in removing until nothing more can be taken away. Every surface, material, and detail must earn its place — and earn it through feeling, not just function.

People first

Spaces exist for the people who live and work inside them. We begin each project by listening, and we finish only when the space feels unmistakably like its owners.

Craft in every detail

The difference between a good space and an extraordinary one is found in the joints, the thresholds, the quality of light at 4 pm. We take the small things seriously.
